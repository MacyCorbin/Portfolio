Memory Games

Choose between the two memory games, Marvel Mix and Marvel Match.

Marvel Mix

Marvel Mix challenges your memory by switching all of the symbols, but you can only select a symbol once! You win the game once you have selected all 12 symbols without clicking twice! Your current game score and your highest score are located on the top right of the page.

Marvel Mix
Marvel Match

Marvel Match hides all of the symbols and only the backside of the card can be seen. You must select a card to view an Avenger's symbol. Match the all of the Avenger pairs to win the game! The current turn and the number of pairs matched are located at top right of the page. The shuffle button will rearrange the cards and all symbols will hidden from view.
